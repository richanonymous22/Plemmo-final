Frequently asked questions

Is approval guaranteed?

No. Funding is subject to lender checks, eligibility, affordability and approval.

Is Plemmo a lender?

No. Plemmo is a credit broker / introducer, not a lender. We introduce businesses to selected funding partners.

What types of businesses can apply?

Many UK businesses may be considered, depending on trading history, turnover, industry and lender criteria.

How fast can funding be arranged?

Timelines vary by lender, documents and approval process.

Can I apply with bad credit?

Some lenders may consider different profiles, but approval is not guaranteed.

How much can I borrow?

Amounts depend on your business profile, affordability, turnover, lender criteria and product type.

Do I need security?

Some options may be unsecured, while others may require security or guarantees depending on lender terms.

Does Plemmo charge a fee?

Plemmo may receive commission from providers. Any customer fees would be clearly disclosed if applicable.

Plemmo is a credit broker, not a lender. Funding is subject to status, eligibility, affordability checks, lender approval and provider terms. Plemmo may receive commission from funding partners.
