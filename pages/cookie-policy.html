Cookie Policy

What cookies and similar technologies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your choices.

Last updated: 10 June 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. "Local storage" works similarly. They help a site function, remember your choices and understand how the site is used. We group them into essential cookies (needed for the site to work) and non-essential cookies (which we only use with your consent).

2. How we use them

Our website is intentionally light on cookies. We currently set only what is necessary to remember your cookie choice. We do not currently use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. If we introduce analytics in future, we will only load it after you consent.

3. Cookies and storage we use

Some pages load resources from trusted third parties to display fonts, icons and animations (for example Google Fonts, Iconify and animation libraries). These providers may process technical data such as your IP address to deliver the content. They do not set tracking cookies on our behalf. Please see the relevant provider's own privacy notice for details.

4. Managing your preferences

You chose your cookie preference using our consent banner when you first visited. You can change your choice at any time using the button below, or by clearing your browser's site data for this website.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings — most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect how the site works. For more about cookies and how to manage them, see the ICO's guidance.

5. More information

For how we handle personal data more generally, see our Privacy Policy. We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time; the "last updated" date shows the latest revision. Questions? Email hello@plemmo.co.uk.
