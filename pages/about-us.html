How we work

An introducer,
on your side

Plemmo introduces businesses to selected third-party providers, and we're not always the direct provider of the products or services shown.

For business funding, Plemmo is a credit broker, not a lender. For card machines, we're an introducer — not a payment processor or acquiring bank.

Plemmo may receive commission from providers. We'll always aim to be clear about how things work so you can decide with confidence.
