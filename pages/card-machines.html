How quickly can I start taking payments?

Timelines vary by provider, approval and delivery. Some providers may offer fast setup and delivery.

Are there hidden fees?

Provider pricing varies. Plemmo helps explain key fees before you proceed.

Contract length depends on the selected provider. Some options may be no-contract or short-term, while others may involve longer agreements.

No. Approval depends on provider checks, business profile and eligibility.
