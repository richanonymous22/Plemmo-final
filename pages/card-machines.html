Frequently asked questions

How quickly can I get a card machine?

Most terminals are dispatched within 24 hours and arrive ready to use. Many businesses are taking payments within 1–3 working days of signing up.

Which provider is right for my business?

It depends on your turnover, average transaction value and the features you need. Use our finder and savings calculator above, or talk to an expert for a tailored recommendation.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. We believe in transparent, blended pricing — no PCI fees, no auth fees and no surprise charges. Everything is laid out clearly before you commit.

What if I need support?

You get UK-based local support whenever you need it. Our team is available by phone and email, and most providers offer 24/7 technical assistance.

Can I keep my existing merchant account?

In many cases yes. We'll review your current setup and let you know the best route — whether that's keeping your account or switching to a better deal.

How do payouts work?

Most providers offer next-day settlement, meaning the money you take today lands in your bank account by the next business morning. No lengthy holding periods.
