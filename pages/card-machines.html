Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to get a card machine?

It depends on the provider, approval and delivery. Some providers may offer fast onboarding and next-working-day delivery.

Are there any monthly fees?

It varies by provider. Some options have no monthly fees, while others include rental or service charges. We'll explain these clearly.

Can I keep my existing merchant account?

This depends on your setup and the chosen provider. We can help check suitable options.

What are the typical transaction fees?

Transaction fees vary by provider, card type and turnover. Indicative rates start from around 0.55% where eligible, subject to approval.

What happens if I want to cancel?

Cancellation and contract terms depend on the provider. We'll help you understand these before you commit.
