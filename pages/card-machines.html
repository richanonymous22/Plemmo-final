FAQ

Card machine questions, answered

Which card machine is best for my business?

It depends on your monthly card turnover, business type, contract preference and settlement needs. Our finder and team help you compare suitable options.

How quickly can I start taking payments?

Setup speed depends on provider approval and delivery. Some providers may offer fast onboarding and next-working-day delivery.

Are there hidden fees?

Provider pricing varies. We help explain the key fees — rental, transaction and settlement — before you proceed.

What is the contract length?

Contract length depends on the provider. Some options may be no-contract or short-term, while others involve longer agreements.

Can I keep my existing merchant account?

Existing merchant account compatibility depends on your setup and the chosen provider. We can help check suitable options.

How do settlements work?

Settlement times vary by provider and account setup. Some options may offer next-day or faster payouts where eligible.

Is approval guaranteed?

No. Approval is not guaranteed — it depends on provider checks, business profile and eligibility.

Does Plemmo process payments directly?

No. Plemmo does not process payments directly — we introduce businesses to selected payment providers.
