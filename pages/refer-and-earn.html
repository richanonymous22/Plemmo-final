FAQs

Common questions

Who can make a referral?

Anyone can refer a UK business to Plemmo — you don't need to be an existing customer. You just need to know a business owner who could benefit from our solutions.

When will I receive my reward?

Your reward is triggered once the referred business signs up for a qualifying Plemmo solution and meets the criteria. We'll notify you and arrange payment.

Is there a limit to how many businesses I can refer?

No — there is no set limit on the number of eligible businesses you can refer. Each qualifying referral generates a separate reward.

What counts as a qualifying referral?

A qualifying referral is a new business (not already known to Plemmo) that signs up for and takes up a Plemmo-introduced solution. Full criteria are set out in our T&Cs.

Does the business I refer know I referred them?

We don't share your details with the business without your consent, but you're welcome to let them know — many referrals work best when the business is expecting our call.
